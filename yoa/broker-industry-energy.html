Energy

From inception, YOA has been focused on meeting the insurance needs of the Nigerian energy industry.

As global demand has soared so as the risks faced by the industry, from exploration in deeper water and wells to sabotage and political risks. The enactment of the Nigerian Content Development law has changed the landscape drastically and compelled us to improve our knowledge and expertise.
We are well equipped to deliver world-class energy risk management solutions that are highly effective against all levels of technical and operational risks in the energy industry. We can develop insurance and reinsurance programmes for all aspects of the exploration and production cycle including:

  • Drilling programmes
  • Offshore construction projects
  • Onshore construction projects
  • Physical damage
  • Business interruption
  • Liabilities
