Insurance

Our dedicated insurance team has extensive knowledge of both the local and global insurance markets.

By combining our awareness of developments in the industry with an understanding of the needs of our clients, we can create innovative insurance solutions that minimises risk to your business. Our team of specialised brokers understand the risks associated with many different industries and processes.

Our insurance services include:
  • Design, placement and servicing for local and global corporations
  • Determining optimum programme structure and levels of self insurance
  • Drafting and reviewing policy wordings - we offer comparisons to existing wordings and can produce bespoke wordings, being mindful of technological trends and the demands of corporate governance
  • Benchmarking premiums and cover against peer companies
  • Driving change and product innovation in the market place
  • Unrivalled claim service to ensure a prompt and satisfactory settlement
