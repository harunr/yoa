Reinsurance

Our reinsurance broking practice provides risk management services to many Nigerian insurers for whom we design and place a variety of reinsurance programmes.

We have a team of professionals with diverse skills who respond to our clients on a range of business and technical issues. We are available to offer advice at the time of a claim.

Facultative

In the facultative market, we empower our clients to better understand and manage risk through our innovative facultative solutions. We provide them with an effective access to both the local and global facultative markets.
Our solutions help our clients to gain a competitive advantage by improving their operational effectiveness and allocation of capital. It is also important to mention that all our solutions satisfy all local insurance regulations.

Treaty

Working with JLT, we have been able to help our clients to effectively manage their risk portfolio through treaty reinsurance. Our treaty programs are flexible, well structured and priced in ways that reflect your underwriting profile and objectives on premium growth and return on capital.

JLT Re experts are client-focused specialists who use industry experience, advanced analytics and strong market relationships to identify suitable markets for clients.
