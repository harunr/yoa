Construction

We are a leading provider of insurance, reinsurance and risk management services to the construction industry.

The risks on major projects tend to be complex and we help clients to identify, quantify, prevent, mitigate, control, and transfer risk. We offer practical and capital-efficient solutions that lead to improved management of costs and processes, which grow the overall value for our clients.
We offer the following products and solutions to contractors, owners, construction professionals and project financiers:

  • Construction All Risks
  • Erection All Risks
  • Advance Loss of Profits/Delay in Start Up
  • Bonds
  • Latent Defects
  • Subcontractor Default insurance
  • Builders Risk
  • Third Party Liability
