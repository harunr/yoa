Our Story

The YOA companies can trace its roots back to 1978, when Yinka Omilani and Associates, Nigeria’s oldest professional loss adjuster, was formed.

In 2002, we formed YOA Insurance Brokers to meet the growing need for Nigerian expertise in oil and gas insurance broking. Since then we have gathered significant experience across a range of sectors, handling some of Nigeria’s most complex insurance deals.
Now, the YOA Group helps local and foreign companies and individuals make informed decisions about how to manage risk. We do this through insurance broking, direct sales and consulting.
